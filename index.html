Common questions

How does coaching work?

We begin with clarity: a conversation about where you are, where you want to be, and what's in the way. From there, we design a structure that fits your schedule and needs—typically 60-minute sessions, bi-weekly or monthly. This isn't therapy. It's practical, psychology-informed work on presence, self-trust, and leadership embodiment.

What's the time commitment?

Most clients work together for 3-6 months, though some engagements are shorter (leadership transition support) or longer (ongoing executive coaching). Sessions are scheduled around your calendar. The work happens both in sessions and in the moments you apply what we discuss.

Do you work with teams or just individuals?

Both. Individual coaching focuses on personal leadership and confidence. For teams and organizations, I design learning programs, facilitate leadership development workshops, and build L&D strategies that scale. The approach stays the same: grounded, practical, human.

How do we start?

Reach out below. We'll have an initial conversation—no pressure, no pitch. If it feels like a fit, we move forward. If not, I'll point you toward resources or people who might serve you better. Clarity first, always.
